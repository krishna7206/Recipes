COOKIES RECIPE

Description

Fast and sort of delicious cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter,softened
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs, 2 teaspoons vanilla extract, 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup dark chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. In a bowl blend sugar, melted butter and brown sugar
  3. Beat in eggs one at a time
  4. Add dry components to the same bowl, mix dough and add in chocolate chips
  5. Bake for about 10 minutes in a preheated oven
