CHICKEN CURRY RECIPE

Description

A chicken curry recipe I came up with when I was quarantined in Memphis,TN. Inspired by Kerela and Maharashtra

Ingredients

  • Boneless Chicken 200 gms
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Coriander
  • Garam Masala
  • Cocunut Milk
  • Cashews
  • Basic Indian Spices

Steps

  1. In a pan, heat a few teaspoons of oil, fry spices onion and garlic for a few mins
  2. Add onions and tomatoes and cook until a bit mushy
  3. Take the mushy mix and blend it with crushed cashews and curd, Set aside
  4. In the same cook the marinated chicken(curd, spices, turmeric powder) and add back the mushy mix alongwith the coconut milk at the end
