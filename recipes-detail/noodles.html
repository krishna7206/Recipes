STIR FRY RECIPE

Description

Simple Weeknight Stir Fry

Ingredients

  • Boneless Chicken 200 gms
  • Vegetables of Choice
  • Garlic,Ginger,Spring Onions
  • Soy Sauce, Stir Fry Sauce(Optional)
  • Rice/Noodles

Steps

  1. In a pan, heat a few teaspoons of oil, fry garlic and Spring Onions
  2. Add Ginger Garlic paste and cook till a bit mushy
  3. Add cut chicken to the same pan, cook for a few mins and stir in sauces of choice
  4. Add Noodles/Rice to the pan, Garnish as desired
